Omelette Recipe

Classic Omelette

Prep

5 mins

Cook

5 mins

Total

10 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
    • room temperature
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Instructions

  • 1. Beat the eggs and milk until light and frothy.
  • 2. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the butter.
  • 3. Pour in the eggs. Let them set for a few seconds.
  • 4. Use a spatula to push the cooked eggs from the edge of the skillet toward the center.
  • 5. When the eggs are mostly set but still soft, fold in half.
  • 6. Slide the omelette onto a plate.

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories: 292kcal
  • Carbohydrates: 1g
  • Protein: 13g
  • Fat: 26g
